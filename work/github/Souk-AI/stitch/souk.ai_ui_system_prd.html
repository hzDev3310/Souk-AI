# Souk.AI UI System Specification (Bootstrap 5) ## Core Branding - **Primary Color (Growth/Leads):** #198754 (Fresh Green) - **Secondary Color (Structure/Text):** #2C4854 (Deep Blue) - **Background:** #F8F9FA (Off-White) - **Surface:** #FFFFFF (White) ## 1. Content & Layout - **Reboot:** Background #F8F9FA, Primary Text #2C4854. - **Typography:** Headings (H1-H6) in Deep Blue. Semi-bold for prices, Medium for titles. Links: Fresh Green (Hover: Deep Blue). - **Images:** 16px border-radius for thumbnails. Circular store logos. - **Tables:** Deep Blue headers with white text. Green-tinted striped rows (5% opacity) for successful leads. - **Figures:** Used for "Product Condition" with small, muted gray "State of Wear" captions. ## 2. Forms & Inputs ("Upload & Contact") - **Form Control:** Extra padding, Fresh Green focus glow. - **Select:** Custom dropdowns for "Category" and "State of Wear". - **Checks/Radios:** Fresh Green fill when selected. - **Range:** Sliders for "Price Range" and "Commission %". - **Input Group:** Search bar with Deep Blue icon button. - **Floating Labels:** Used for Login/Register. - **Validation:** Green checkmarks for valid, Red for errors. ## 3. Navigation - **Navbar (Desktop):** White with Deep Blue bottom border. Includes logo, search, and Seller/Influencer toggle. - **Navs & Tabs:** Used for Description, Store Info, and Shipping. - **Navbar (Mobile):** Fixed white bottom bar with 5 icons. Center "Post" icon is a Fresh Green circle. - **Breadcrumb:** Deep Blue text. - **Pagination:** Fresh Green themed. ## 4. Interactive Elements - **Accordion:** FAQ section. - **Alerts:** Fresh Green for success, Deep Blue for info. - **Badge:** "State of Wear" indicators on product image corners. - **Buttons:** - **Primary:** Deep Blue ("View Details", "Edit Profile"). - **Success:** Fresh Green ("Contact on WhatsApp"). - **Card:** White box, 16px radius, contains image, price, and "Referral" badge. - **Carousel:** Image gallery for products. - **List Group:** Admin Sidebar with Green active states. - **Modal/Toasts:** "Share Link" popups and success notifications. - **Progress:** Green bar for "Reward Tier". - **Spinners:** Fresh Green for uploads.
